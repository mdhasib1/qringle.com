About Us

What is Qringle?

Qringle is a fresh, friendly social chat app built for people who miss the simple joy of online conversations.
No algorithms. No followers. Just real people connecting in real time.

We believe the best part of the internet wasn’t scrolling — it was talking.

Why We Exist

The internet used to feel smaller. Chat rooms brought strangers together around shared interests, not just trending content. Qringle is a throwback to that era — reimagined for today.

  • Join public chat rooms by topic or location
  • Meet new people instantly
  • Have real conversations without pressure
  • Discover fun, spontaneous connections

What Makes Qringle Different

  • → Rooms that feel alive
    Rooms light up when people join. Activity is real-time, not curated.
  • → Simple privacy controls
    Block anyone instantly. Choose who can message you.
  • → Premium features only if you want them
    Most of Qringle is completely free. Premium is optional for extra perks.
  • → No fake engagement tricks
    No follower counts. No like farms. No influencer culture. Just chat.

Who We Are

Qringle is independently built and run by a solo developer with one simple goal:

→ Make the internet feel human again.

This isn’t a giant tech company. There are no investors pulling strings. Just a builder who loves creating spaces where people can talk freely.

Our Mission

Connect the world — one chat room at a time
